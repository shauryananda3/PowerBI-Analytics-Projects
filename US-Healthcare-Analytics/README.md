# 🏥 US Healthcare Analytics Report

## 📌 Project Overview
Developed a multi-page Power BI report to analyze hospital performance, patient trends, treatment efficiency, and operational KPIs.

## 🎯 Business Objective
Help healthcare leadership improve patient outcomes, optimize hospital resources, and monitor operational efficiency.

## 📊 Dashboard Pages

### 1. Executive Summary
- Total Patients
- Revenue
- Average Stay Duration
- Readmission Rate

### 2. Department Performance
- Cardiology vs Oncology vs Emergency
- Avg treatment time
- Satisfaction scores

### 3. Patient Insights
- Age group analysis
- Gender split
- Insurance coverage

## 📈 KPIs Used
- Patient Count
- Occupancy Rate
- Avg Length of Stay
- Readmission %
- Revenue per Patient

## 🛠 Tools Used
- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX
- Excel

## 💡 Key Insights
- Emergency department had highest wait times
- Cardiology generated highest revenue
- Senior patients showed higher readmission %
